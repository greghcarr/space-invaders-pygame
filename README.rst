Space Invaders Pygame
#####################

"Space Invaders" clone made with `pygame-ce
<https://pypi.org/project/pygame-ce/>`_.

.. image:: ./space_invaders_pygame_gameplay.gif
   :alt: video of Space Invaders Pygame gameplay
   :width: 300px

How to play:
~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Press the 'A' or Left Arrow key and the 'D' or Right Arrow key to move your ship.
* Press Spacebar to shoot.
* Don't get hit by an enemy bullet, or you'll explode and lose a life!
* Lose all three of your starting lives, or allow the enemies to touch your ship, and it's game over.
* Press the 'N' key to start a new game!

Features:
~~~~~~~~~
* Sprites loaded from a spritesheet.
* Rudimentary animated sprites.
* Infinite levels with scaling difficulty.
* High score.
